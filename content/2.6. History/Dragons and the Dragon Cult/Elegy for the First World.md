[[Tiamat]]
[[Bahamut]]

Breathe, Dragons; Sing of the First World
forged out of Chaos and painted with beauty.
Sing of Bahamut, the Platinum,
moulding the shape of the mountains and rivers;
Sing too of Chromatic Tiamat,
painting all over the infinite canvas.
Partnered they woke in the darkness;
partnered, they laboured in acts of creation.

Breathe, Dragons; Sing then of Vokun
shadow-black absence that fathered their likeness;
Vokun, first born of dragonkind
laboured alongside Bahamut and Tiamat,
Shaping the dragon they crafted;
dragons metallic and dragons chromatic.
Breathe, dragons - draw in the undying-soul
gifted unto you at the dawn of creation.

Breathe, dragons; sing of the outsiders,
war-bringer gods with their mortal adherents;
Teeming, they came to the First World at its end,
conquering deities seized their victory
Fallen was Vokun the World-Eater,
betrayed by his metallic-child before world  reborn,
Taken to the Hells, by maw
Sequestered, subdued and stored.

Breathe, dragons; sing of Tiamat,
raging in battle with no hope of victory.
She ate the shadow-father's soul,
captive to gods laying claim to creation.
Sing of her sacrifice, her vengeance
securing a position as keeper and care-taker
Five-Headed, Monstrous, and Mighty
following a campaign of destruction and resurrection 

Breathe, dragons; sing of Bahamut,
maker of treacherous peace with the outsider deities.
Welcomed to mountains celestial, 
worshipped by some as the Platinum Paladin.
Protector of the lesser races, 
honour bound traitor to his own
Capitulated seventh crowned king,
ruling the wreck of the First World's devastation.
