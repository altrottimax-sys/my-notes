# Dragon Barrow
 
The dragon that besets us is not the first to threaten this region. Between here and Neverwinter lies the barrow mound of a warrior whose magical dragon-slaying sword helped fell a green dragon terrorizing the High Road a century ago. Rumor has it the [dragon slayer](app://obsidian.md/3-Mechanics/CLI/items/dragon-slayer.md) sword is buried there, too. Retrieve it, and let the sword be its own reward!
